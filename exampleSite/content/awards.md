## AMR Impact Award
**Vivli AMR Surveillance Data Challenge** | Sep 2025

Received the top award for significant impact in Vivli's global AMR surveillance data challenge, competing against **58 teams from 22 countries** to reuse industry AMR surveillance data (Vivli AMR Register) for novel insights. The challenge was funded by Johnson & Johnson, Paratek, Pfizer, and the U.S. NIH (GREI).
